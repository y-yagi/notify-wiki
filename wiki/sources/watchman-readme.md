## What it claims

- "Watchman exists to watch files and record when they actually change. It
  can also trigger actions (such as rebuilding assets) when matching files
  change."
- Maintained by the source control team at Meta; officially supports
  Windows, macOS, and recent Ubuntu/Fedora Linux, with Python/Rust/
  JavaScript clients. FreeBSD and Solaris support is community-maintained,
  not official.
